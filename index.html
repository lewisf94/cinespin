Setup

One quick setup step

CineWheel needs a free Firebase project to store your club's data. It takes about five to ten minutes, once.

  1. Go to console.firebase.google.com and create a project.
  2. Add a Web app and copy the firebaseConfig values.
  3. Open Build, then Firestore Database and create a database.
  4. Paste the contents of firestore.rules into the Firestore Rules tab and publish.
  5. Open Build, then Authentication, then Sign-in method and enable Anonymous.
  6. Paste your config into js/firebase.js and reload.

Full instructions are in README.md.
