CineClub

About & Credits

CineClub is a free hobby project, supported by optional donations.

CineClub is a film-club app: a group adds films to a wheel, takes turns spinning to pick the week's film, sets a watch-by deadline, then everyone marks it watched and leaves a sealed half-star rating and review. Reviews unseal and the turn passes once every member has watched and rated.

It's built and run by Lewis Fowler. Questions, feedback or bug reports are welcome at hello@thecineclub.co.uk.

Film data & credits

This product uses TMDB and the TMDB APIs but is not endorsed, certified, or otherwise approved by TMDB.

Film posters, titles, release years, runtimes, genres and recommendations are provided by The Movie Database (TMDB).

"Where to watch" / streaming availability is sourced from JustWatch (via TMDB). All rights in that data remain with their respective owners.
